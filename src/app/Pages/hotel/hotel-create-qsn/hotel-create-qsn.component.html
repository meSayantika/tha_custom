Restaurants & Bars

The options for CHAT or RESERVATION "PER RESTAURANT" are INCLUDED in your platform package. -We can integrate your current RESERVATION APP (OpenTable, Resy, Book a Table, etc.)
-We can link the RESERVATION button to a CALL or CHAT communication.
